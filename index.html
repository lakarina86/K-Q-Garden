Welcome to Karen and Quentin's Garden !

Here you can find some helpful information on how to grow beautiful plants. Including tips on how to maintain them alive during winter !

Basil (Genovese)

Basil grows well in containers indoors at any time of year provided you can supply enough light. Use any rich, loose, well drained soil.

Start 6-8 before the last frost in spring

70° F

Soil must be kept warm

7-14 days

Mint (Peppermint)

Mint is so hardy and tough that it will grow right back. Dry the leaves and flowers for peppermint tea. The flowers are edible and make salads and sweets come to life.

Start 8-10 before the last frost in spring

70° F

7-14 days

Tomato (Cherry)

Tomatoes like fertile, well drained soil that is high in organic matter. Add 1 cup complete organic fertilizer beneath each transplant. Harvest when the fruit is the desired colour.

Start mid-March to early April

50° F

7-14 days
